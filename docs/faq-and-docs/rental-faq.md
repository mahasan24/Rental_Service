# Rentel Service – FAQ

## Booking

- **How do I book a van?** Sign up or log in, choose a van from our fleet, pick your dates, and confirm the booking. You can also use "How can we help?" on the home page to get van recommendations based on your needs.
- **What if I need to cancel?** Go to My Bookings and click Cancel on the booking. Cancellation is free up to 24 hours before the start date.
- **Can I extend my rental?** Contact support with your booking ID to request an extension, subject to availability.
- **How far in advance should I book?** We recommend booking at least 3 days in advance, especially for Large vans during peak moving season (summer months).
- **Can I modify my booking dates?** Currently you need to cancel the existing booking and create a new one with the updated dates.

## Pricing

- **How is the price calculated?** Prices are per day. The total is (price per day) × (number of days). You see the daily rate on each van's detail page.
- **Are there extra fees?** The quoted price is for the van rental only. Optional extras (e.g. insurance, GPS) may be added later.
- **Do you offer discounts for longer rentals?** Rentals of 7 days or more may qualify for a discount. Contact support for details.
- **What payment methods do you accept?** We accept all major credit cards and debit cards.

## Fleet

- **What van types do you have?** We offer Small (compact, 2 people), Medium (standard, 4-5 people), and Large (moving, 6-8 people) vans. Use our recommendation tool to find the best fit for "moving house", "day trip", etc.
- **Is fuel included?** The van is provided with a full tank. Return it with a full tank or pay the difference.
- **What is included with the van?** Each van comes with basic insurance, a full tank of fuel, and standard equipment. Large vans include tie-downs and some have hydraulic lifts.
- **Are the vans automatic or manual?** Most of our fleet is automatic. Check the van specs on the detail page for specifics.
- **What is the minimum rental period?** The minimum rental is 1 day.

## Account

- **How do I create an account?** Click "Sign up" in the navigation bar, enter your email, name, and password, and you're ready to go.
- **I forgot my password.** Password reset is coming soon. For now, contact support for help.
- **Can I view my past bookings?** Yes, go to "My Bookings" after logging in to see all current and past bookings.

## Support

- **How can I get help?** Use the FAQ bot on this site for instant answers, or contact support via the email listed in the footer.
- **What are your support hours?** Our FAQ bot is available 24/7. Human support is available Monday-Friday, 9am-5pm.
