# About Rentel Service

## Company

Rentel Service.com is a rental van booking platform that makes it easy to find and book the right van for any occasion. Whether you're moving house, taking a day trip, or transporting furniture, we have a van to suit your needs.

## How It Works

1. **Browse**: Visit our van listing page to see available vans. Filter by type (Small, Medium, Large), capacity, and price.
2. **Choose**: Click on a van to see its full details, specs, and features.
3. **Book**: Select your dates, review the price summary, and confirm your booking. You'll need to be logged in.
4. **Go**: Pick up your van on the start date and enjoy your rental.

## Smart Recommendations

Not sure which van you need? Use the "How can we help?" section on the home page. Tell us what you're doing (e.g. "moving house", "day trip", "furniture delivery") and we'll recommend the best van type for you. If you've booked before, we'll also consider your rental history.

## AI Features

- **FAQ Bot**: Our intelligent FAQ bot can answer your questions about booking, pricing, fleet, and more. Click the "?" button in the bottom-right corner.
- **Van Descriptions**: Van descriptions are generated using AI based on the van's specifications, ensuring accurate and engaging descriptions.

## Technology

Rentel is built with React (frontend), Node.js/Express (backend), and PostgreSQL (database). AI features are powered by LangChain and OpenAI/Gemini APIs for retrieval-augmented generation (RAG).
